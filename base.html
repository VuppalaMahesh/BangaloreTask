About Icicle

Icicle builds Web & Mobile products for Startups & Companies helping in bringing their ideas to life.

We design & build Web applications, Mobile apps using Elixir, Phoenix, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React Native for Android & iOS.

We have been building products for our Clients since 2004. Our relationships with our clients are a testimony to the kind of value we bring in. Average span of our client engagement is over 24 months, the longest about 8 years and still active. Our focus on the Lean Startup model, Agile Project Management and Continuous Deployment allows us to shorten product go-live date, enable scalable architecture, keeping project costs & delays at minimum.

Technologies we offer

With over a decade of development background, we work with open technologies like Elixir, Phoenix Framework, Ruby on Rails, Ember.js, React.js, React Native for Android, iOS etc. in bringing ideas to life for our clients and partners.
